Attributes are named values you define once and reuse throughout a document.

This guide was written by {author-name} and targets {product} {version}.

== Built-in attributes

Asciidoctor sets many attributes for you, such as the current date
({docdate}) and time ({doctime}).

== Custom attributes

Define your own with `:name: value` in the header, then reference them
with curly braces. For example, the documentation lives at {url-docs}.

== Attributes from the extension

Attributes configured through the `asciidoc.preview.attributes` setting are also
resolved in the preview. This one comes from your settings: {application-name}.
